February Newsletter

The month's Self-Care newsletter features the best breakfast comfort food, mellow tunes for February showers, feel good shows that make you laugh, and tips that help schedule this months activities.

- Comfort Food -

Cinnamon French Toast

For a good start to a rainy day, make yout french toast with a dash of vanilla and a sprinkle of cinnamon. This simple meal takes less than thirty minutes. Once those bread slices are golden brown on both sides, the smell will remind of that good ol’ home cooking.


- Music -

Drake's More Life Album

More Life is a very complex evolution on Drake's previous music style. The album is often referred to as a mixtape because Drake weaves organic conversations into every track on the album. Some voices on the track include Lil Wayne, Zoe Kravitz, and DJ Moodyman. More Life’s soft buoyant sound gives a mellow feel to listeners. Top tracks include: Passionfruit, Blem, Teenage Fever, and Glow (ft. Kanye West).

- Watch List -

The Golden Girls

Four women, Dorothy, Rose, Blanche, and Sophia, navigate their golden years in their Miami home. Together they experience the highs and lows of a mature life with their blossoming friendships. The character’s will pull at your heart strings and make you laugh in the space of the same minute. By the end of the third episode, you’ll memorize the shows theme song, “Thank You for Being a Friend”.

- Activities -

Schedule Scrapbooking

Scrapbooking isn’t always easy, but the NYC Muji Store has great books and pens that can help craft a stylized planner that fits your weekly and daily needs. With some inspirational help from Pinterest, a few brightly colored markers, and some time, you’ll have your own planner that looks awesome.
